Welcome to my little corner of the internet!

Born and raised in Zimbabwe, a true African at heart, I care deeply about people. After studying psychology in the Netherlands, I decided I want something a bit different.

*Enter software engineering.*

I've always been curious about the way the world works, and not just how it works, but why it works that way. And so, I fell in love with software engineering when I realised how much it allows me to be myself - it is so logical, and there is an explanation for almost everything, it fulfils my love of maths and physics, all while scratching my creative itch. I'm also interested in how tech can help the people I see around me, and how tech allows us to build things to solve real world problems.

I was very blessed to enrol in the Generation Tech Talent program, which has given me the opportunity to learn and implement in practice languages such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript (and TypeScript for good measure!), as well as types of programming such as functional programming, and objected-oriented programming, and source control with Git. We also learnt about how to be and think like software engineers, developing a growth mindset, cultivating perseverance, and personal responsibility. I also met some really great humans, and got to work on some pretty great projects!
